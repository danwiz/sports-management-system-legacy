# Provenance and Attribution

## Origin

The legacy Sports Management System was developed as University of Technology,
Jamaica coursework during 2005–2006 and supplied by Dane Wisdom for archival
preservation and modernization.

## Preserved evidence

- Four closely related legacy C++ source revisions are retained under `legacy-source/`.
- The original assignment and user-acceptance feedback are retained under `docs/original/`.
- Extracted text, checksums, technical assessments, and modernization records are retained separately.

## Preservation rule

Legacy files are historical evidence. Modern work must be added under the
portable implementation or documentation areas and must not silently rewrite
or normalize the originals.
